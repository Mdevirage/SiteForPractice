In recent years, there has been a growing interest in retro games and remakes of classic titles. Many gamers are eager to immerse themselves in the atmosphere of past games. With the development of technology and the increasing power of modern devices, there is a need to adapt old games to new platforms and standards. Ports of classic games not only help preserve the cultural heritage of the industry but also attract a new generation of players who may not have had the opportunity to experience the old, now-forgotten projects.

This website is dedicated to the porting of Shadow of the Beast, originally released for the Japanese FM-Towns computer. It showcases the history of the porting process and the current stages of development.
